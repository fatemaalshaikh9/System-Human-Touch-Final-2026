About Human Touch

Human Touch is a smart companion application created to improve daily life and independence for people with disabilities. The application provides reminders, AI communication, emergency support, accessible guidance, health support, and volunteer services in one easy-to-use platform.

Key Features

⏰

Smart Reminders

Medication, meal, and appointment reminders to support daily routines.

🤖

AI Communication

Smart communication support using AI, voice input, and text-to-speech.

🚨

Emergency Support

Quick emergency help and emergency contact access.

📍

Accessible Guidance

Helps users find accessible places and useful services.

🤝

Volunteer Help

Connects users with volunteers for assistance and support.

🌙

Accessibility Options

Includes dark mode, large text, and bilingual support.

Problem Statement

People with disabilities may face challenges in communication, health management, emergency situations, and accessing suitable services. Human Touch aims to simplify daily life and improve independence through smart assistive technologies.

Project Objectives

01

Support users in managing daily routines independently.

02

Improve communication using AI and accessibility tools.

03

Provide fast emergency support and assistance.

04

Connect patients, companions, and volunteers together.

Results

✔ Developed a complete assistive mobile application for people with disabilities.

✔ Implemented AI-powered communication and accessibility support.

✔ Created emergency support and volunteer assistance features.

✔ Added reminder systems for medication, meals, and appointments.

Conclusion & Future Work

Human Touch successfully combines accessibility, AI communication, emergency services, reminders, and volunteer support into one mobile application.

Future improvements may include wearable device integration, smart health monitoring, additional AI capabilities, and more accessibility services.
